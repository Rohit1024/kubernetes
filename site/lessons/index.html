Lessons¶

Welcome to the lessons section. Here, you will learn the core concepts and architecture of Kubernetes step by step.

Lesson Outline¶

  1. Lesson 1: Pod Anatomy & CrashLoopBackOff
    • Understanding control plane vs. worker node components.
    • Diagnosing why a container repeatedly crashes.
  2. Lesson 2: Node Scheduling, Deployment Strategies & Autoscaling
    • Labels, selectors, and scheduling decisions.
    • Rolling updates vs. Recreate strategies.
  3. Lesson 3: Service Communication, DNS & Troubleshooting
    • Service discovery and core DNS.
    • Internal cluster networking.
  4. Lesson 4: Stateless/Stateful App Configuration & Secrets
    • Environment variables, ConfigMaps, and Secrets.
  5. Lesson 5: Ingress & GKE Load Balancing
    • Routing HTTP/HTTPS traffic to services.
  6. Lesson 6: Persistent Volumes, PVCs & StorageClasses
    • Dynamic provisioning and storage options.
  7. Lesson 7: GKE Gateway API
    • Modern routing and multi-tenant traffic management.
  8. Lesson 8: Resource Limits, Probes & Graceful Shutdown
    • CPU/Memory requests, limits, and health checks.
  9. Lesson 9: Capstone Project
    • Deploying a complete multi-tier application.
  10. Lesson 10: Helm Package Manager
    • Packaging and deploying complex Kubernetes apps.
