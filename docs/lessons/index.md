# Lessons

Welcome to the lessons section. Here, you will learn the core concepts and architecture of Kubernetes step by step.

## Lesson Outline

1. **[Lesson 1: Pod Anatomy & CrashLoopBackOff](0001-pod-anatomy-and-crashloopbackoff.md)**
    - Understanding control plane vs. worker node components.
    - Diagnosing why a container repeatedly crashes.
2. **[Lesson 2: Node Scheduling, Deployment Strategies & Autoscaling](0002-node-scheduling-deployment-strategies-autoscaling.md)**
    - Labels, selectors, and scheduling decisions.
    - Rolling updates vs. Recreate strategies.
3. **[Lesson 3: Service Communication, DNS & Troubleshooting](0003-service-communication-dns-troubleshooting.md)**
    - Service discovery and core DNS.
    - Internal cluster networking.
4. **[Lesson 4: Stateless/Stateful App Configuration & Secrets](0004-stateless-stateful-secrets-gcp.md)**
    - Environment variables, ConfigMaps, and Secrets.
5. **[Lesson 5: Ingress & GKE Load Balancing](0005-ingress-gke-load-balancing.md)**
    - Routing HTTP/HTTPS traffic to services.
6. **[Lesson 6: Persistent Volumes, PVCs & StorageClasses](0006-pv-pvc-storageclasses.md)**
    - Dynamic provisioning and storage options.
7. **[Lesson 7: GKE Gateway API](0007-gke-gateway-api.md)**
    - Modern routing and multi-tenant traffic management.
8. **[Lesson 8: Resource Limits, Probes & Graceful Shutdown](0008-resources-probes-graceful-shutdown.md)**
    - CPU/Memory requests, limits, and health checks.
9. **[Lesson 9: Capstone Project](0009-capstone-project.md)**
    - Deploying a complete multi-tier application.
10. **[Lesson 10: Helm Package Manager](0010-helm-package-manager.md)**
    - Packaging and deploying complex Kubernetes apps.
